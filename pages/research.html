Rotodynamic Analysis of Cryogenic Turbopumps Rotor Supported by Metal Foil Bearing in Ansys

Advisors: Prof. Suraj Kumar Behera & Dr. Ajit Kumar Sahu (Oct 22’- May 23’)
Objective:- Enhance cryogenic turbopump performance with metal foil bearings.
Simulating the rotordynamic analysis in Ansys with the applied boundary conditions(location of the bearings, Stiffness of bearings, Damping of bearings, unbalanced load, speed of the rotor) to predict the feasibility of the turbopumps. For the implementation of this project, Solidworks and Ansys ®. software has been used.M
